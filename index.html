We are Ocarime, a network of composers, sound designers and software developers. Our collaborative is composed of different audio specialists, creating a unique network to deliver carefully designed audio for both commercial and personal use.

Our team consists of six specialists who are active in the audio industry. The work we do includes game audio, music, sound design, audio integration and software development regarding audio. With many expertises we can decide on a per-project basis who is best suited for the required project roles, so that we can deliver the best results.

The team

Bastiaan van Bentum

As a composer and sound designer, Bastiaan focuses on combining these two disciplines to create coherent sonic worlds in every project. His style varies in the use of different combinations of organic and synthetic sound elements.

Greg Candel

Greg believes in the importance of context and quality of sound in media. By combining knowledge, experience, industry-standard tools and software, he strives to deliver designed audio while developing a strong relationship with clients.

gregcandel.com

Danae Dekker

Danae is specialized in the integration of music and technology. She is fascinated by musical storytelling in a fantastical and whimsical setting, which she applies in autonomous compositions, games and interactive media. Moreover she has a broad knowledge of software and coding skills to implement music and audio in any interactive project.

danaedekker.com

Stijn de Koning

Stijn is a media composer and sound designer for games and films and an (audio) software programmer. He combines his skills in these different fields in his work and enjoys finding new exciting ways to produce professional audio and software.

sdkoning.com

Thomas van Rossum

Thomas is a composer and sound designer for games, who works with an emphasis on virtual orchestration. His conceptual way of working helps to provide a unique and fitting score for the projects at hand. His previous works contain a wide variety of different genres, with tracks varying from heavily orchestrated soundtracks, to experimental soundscapes.

thomasmusicdesign.com

Amber Veerman

With a classical background Amber is mainly focused on the compositions regarding to the orchestra audio within games, animations and movies. Throughout the years she has progressed from the emotional piano pieces to the dramatic, yet fitting, orchestra pieces that will move the heart from one emotion to the other.

amberveerman.com
